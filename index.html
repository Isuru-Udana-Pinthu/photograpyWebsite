Our Story

Tilia is a Wedding & Portrait Studio based in New York

We are a team of passionate photographers and videographers dedicated to capturing the most beautiful moments in your life. With over a decade of experience, we blend artistry and technical excellence to create images that will be treasured for generations.

From intimate portrait sessions to grand wedding celebrations, we approach every project with the same level of care, creativity, and commitment to perfection.
