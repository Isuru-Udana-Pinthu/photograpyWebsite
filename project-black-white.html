The Series

When Light Meets Shadow

This lifestyle series strips away color to reveal the raw essence of the human experience against breathtaking landscapes. Shot across the dunes of Morocco, these images explore solitude, scale, and the quiet courage it takes to wander into the unknown. Every shadow tells a story that color would only complicate.
